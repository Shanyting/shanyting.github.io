    Rainforest Facts!

  • Rainforests only cover around 2% of the total surface area of Earth, but are home to half of all the species on Earth.
  • Rainforests help regulate the temperatures and weather patterns in the world.
  • 1/5 of our fresh water is found in tropical rainforests (the Amazon Basin).
  • 70% or more of the plants that are used to treat cancer are only found in tropical rainforests.
  • About 1/4 of natural medicines have been discovered in rainforests.
  • In 4 square miles of a tropical rainforest, you will find 1,500 flowering plant species and 750 tree species.
  • The Amazon rainforest is the largest tropical rainforest in the world!
  • There used to be around 6 million square miles of rainforest in the beginning, but due to deforestation, there is only half amount of that left.
  • We lose over 80,000 football fields worth of rainforest EVERY SINGLE DAY.
