OUR BEAUTIFUL RAINFORESTS

Rainforests play a priceless role in sustaining life, but every year, large portions of them are cut down for logging, mining, and cattle ranches.

The kingdom of the rainforest contains millions of species, many of which have not been discovered, with over 5 million species that call the rainforest home.
